Emergency Contacts

If you're in an unsafe situation, use the contacts below for immediate help:

  • Police: 100
  • Women Helpline: 1091
  • Fire: 101
  • National Emergency: 112

Safety Tips

  • Always share your location with trusted friends or family.
  • Avoid dark and isolated areas when alone.
  • Carry a personal safety tool like pepper spray.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts.
  • Use safety apps to alert your contacts during emergencies.

Need Help?

If you feel unsafe or need advice, email us or visit your nearest support center.
